Instructions

about the game

In this game you are going to play against the computer,

in this game you are red paddle and the computer is orange paddle, in this game your goal is to earn 4 points to win, if computer scored 4 points from you, you will loose the game.



how to play this game

To play this game you have to move your hand up and down,

if you move your hand up , so the red paddle will move up and if you move your hand down , so the red paddle will move down.
